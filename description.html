Project Aloysius is an unofficial addon for Enigmatica 2: Expert.

It keeps the original 650 quests to guide agents along the way.

This addon is made specifically for the server of the same name hosted on Minecraft 1.12.2

Gated progression: At every Technology and Magic tier there are several different mods to progress through, ultimately leading to the top-tier materials required for the end-game.

Quests: There are currently over 650 quests, that guide you through the progression. You can see most of them in the Images tab.

Space exploration: you'll have the opportunity to explore custom worlds.


Tougher mobs, dragons, sea serpents, zombies with quantum armor, and many more unique threats to make the exploration playstyle more interesting, and rewarding.

With the original modpack being labeled as 'Expert', you can expect tons and tons of custom recipes, increasing the lifespan of your playthrough.

Automation: Applied Energistics 2, Project Red, OpenComputers, Computronics

Tech: Mekanism, NuclearCraft, Thermal Expansion, Ender IO, Immersive Engineering, Railcraft, Draconic Evolution, Industrial Craft 2

Magic: Astral Sorcery, Thaumcraft, Botania, Blood Magic, Psi

Adventure: Twilight Forest, RFTools Dimensions and custom planets & solar systems.

Building: CB Multipart, Engineer's Decor & Doors, Chisels & Bits, Chisel, Building Gadgets, BiblioCraft, ArchitectureCraft, BlockCraftery
